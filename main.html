Welcome to Network Green

About Network Green

Network Green is a platform dedicated to promoting sustainable practices and connecting restaurants with food donation programs. We strive to reduce food wastage in Austin, help the community, and support local businesses.

How It Works

Our platform connects restaurants that have surplus food with food pantries and homeless shelters in need. We provide a seamless way for restaurants to donate their excess food, ensuring that it reaches those who need it the most.

Why Donate

By donating food, restaurants not only contribute to reducing food waste but also make a positive impact on the lives of individuals facing food insecurity. Join us in making Austin a more sustainable and compassionate community.

Get Involved

Whether you're a restaurant owner, a food pantry representative, or an individual passionate about our cause, there are many ways to get involved. Contact us to learn more about volunteer opportunities, partnerships, and donations.
