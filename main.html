Welcome to Network Green

Network Green is a non-profit organization that allows places with food insecurity to request food from places such as restaurants. We want to help reduce food wastage in Austin and help the community, give restautants an incentive to donate their extra food, and help food pantries and homeless shelters. We are choosing to work as a nonprofit to benefit the Austin community as much as we can.
